Crochet Cat Keychain

  • Materials: Cotton yarn in desired colors (body, face, ears), 3mm hook, fiberfill, keychain ring, safety eyes, black embroidery thread.
  • Head (worked in the round):
    R1: MR, 6 sc (6)
    R2: inc in each st around (12)
    R3: (sc, inc) x 6 (18)
    R4: (2 sc, inc) x 6 (24)
    R5–8: sc around (24)
    R9: (2 sc, dec) x 6 (18)
    R10: (sc, dec) x 6 (12)
    Insert eyes, stuff head.
    R11: dec x 6 (6), FO and close.
  • Ears (make 2):
    R1: MR, 4 sc
    R2: (sc, inc) x 2 (6)
    R3: sc around, FO and leave tail to sew.
  • Muzzle (optional):
    With white yarn, ch 4
    R1: sc in 2nd ch, sc, 3 sc in last ch, rotate and sc back down, 2 sc in last st (8)
    FO, embroider nose and mouth.
  • Assembly: Sew ears on top. Attach muzzle (or embroider directly on head). Add whiskers with black thread. Attach keychain ring.
  • Optional: Add blush with pink yarn or embroidery floss for cheeks!
