Amigurumi Bunny

Materials:

  • Worsted weight yarn (pink or white)
  • 3.5mm crochet hook
  • Safety eyes
  • Stuffing
  • Tapestry needle
  • Stitch marker

Head & Body:

  • R1: Magic ring, 6 sc
  • R2: inc in each st (12)
  • R3: (sc, inc) x6 (18)
  • R4: (2 sc, inc) x6 (24)
  • R5–10: sc around (24)
  • R11: (2 sc, dec) x6 (18)
  • R12: (sc, dec) x6 (12)
  • Insert eyes & stuffing
  • R13: dec x6 (6), fasten off

Ears (make 2):

  • R1: Magic ring, 6 sc
  • R2: (sc, inc) x3 (9)
  • R3–7: sc around (9), fasten off

Arms & Legs (make 4):

  • R1: Magic ring, 6 sc
  • R2–5: sc around (6)
  • Stuff lightly, fasten off

Assembly:

  • Sew ears to top of head
  • Attach arms and legs to body
  • Add nose and blush details
