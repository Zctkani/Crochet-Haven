Squid Game Doll Keychain

Materials Needed

  • Worsted weight yarn (Yellow, Orange, Black, White)
  • Crochet hook (Size 3.5mm or E)
  • Fiberfill stuffing
  • Safety eyes or black yarn for embroidery
  • Keychain ring
  • Tapestry needle, scissors

Instructions

Head (Yellow):

  1. 6 sc in magic ring
  2. Inc around (12)
  3. (Sc, inc) around (18)
  4. Sc around for 5 rounds (R4–R8)
  5. Insert safety eyes, stuff the head
  6. (Sc, dec) around (12)
  7. Dec around, fasten off and weave in tail

Body (Orange):

  1. 6 sc in magic ring
  2. Inc around (12)
  3. Sc around for 3 rounds (R3–R5)
  4. Stuff lightly
  5. Dec around (6), close and fasten off

Arms (Make 2):

  1. 4 sc in magic ring
  2. Sc around for 2 rounds
  3. Fasten off and sew to sides of body

Legs (Make 2):

  1. 5 sc in magic ring
  2. Sc around for 2 rounds
  3. Fasten off and sew to base of body

Hair & Face Details:

Use black yarn to embroider the hair fringe and white for the collar.

Final Step:

Attach the keychain ring securely to the top of the head.

Tips

  • Use a stitch marker to track rounds.
  • Secure parts firmly if making as a keychain.
  • Add blush using pink yarn or fabric paint for cheeks!
