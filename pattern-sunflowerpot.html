Sunflower Pot Pattern

  • Step 1: Create the sunflower center with brown yarn: Magic ring, 6 SC. Increase to 18 SC. Make 2 and stuff.
  • Step 2: Petals with yellow yarn: (CH 5, SC, HDC, DC, HDC, SC) in each stitch around the circle.
  • Step 3: Make the stem using green yarn: CH 10, SC back. Fold or roll for thickness.
  • Step 4: Pot base with pink or brown yarn: Magic ring, increase to form a rounded pot. Work up without increases.
  • Step 5: Stuff lightly and insert sunflower stem into the pot. Sew to secure.
  • Optional: Add cute leaves: CH 6, SC to DC then back, sew near the stem or pot rim.
