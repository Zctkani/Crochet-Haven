Crochet Hair Clip

  • Step 1: Crochet a base oval (CH 10, SC around to form a round shape with increases).
  • Step 2: Work 3–4 rows around the base to make it thick enough for a clip backing.
  • Step 3: Decorate with small 3D flowers and leaf details — use magic rings for flowers and CH + SL ST for stems.
  • Step 4: Make multiple styles (like bows, blossoms, or layered ovals) and sew them together if stacking.
  • Step 5: Stitch on a hair clip or snap backing to secure the crochet piece.
  • Step 6: Add finishing borders using SC or shell stitch for a neat edge.
