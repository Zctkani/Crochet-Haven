Cozy Scarf Pattern

  • Step 1: Start with a foundation chain of 25 stitches using a 5mm hook and soft acrylic yarn.
  • Step 2: Work 1 double crochet in the 3rd chain from the hook and across the row.
  • Step 3: Chain 2, turn your work. Double crochet in each stitch across. Repeat this row until the scarf reaches about 60 inches.
  • Step 4: Fasten off and weave in the yarn ends using a tapestry needle.
  • Step 5: For fringe, cut yarn strands about 8 inches long, group them, and attach to each end of the scarf.
