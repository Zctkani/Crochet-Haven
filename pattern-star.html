BINI Logo Star Pattern

Description

This flat crochet star is inspired by the BINI logo design. It’s perfect as an applique, bookmark, or keychain charm. No stuffing needed!

Materials

  • Worsted weight yarn (in your preferred BINI color)
  • 3.5 mm crochet hook
  • Yarn needle & scissors
  • Optional: keyring, safety pin, or glue (for attaching)

Instructions

Flat Star Pattern:
R1: Magic ring, ch 1, 10 sc in ring. Join with sl st. (10 sts)
R2: [Sc in next st, 2 sc in next] around. Join. (15 sts)
R3: *Ch 4, sl st in 2nd ch from hook, sc in next, hdc in next,
skip 2 sts, sl st in next*. Repeat from * to * 5 times to form points.
Fasten off and weave in ends.

Optional Uses

  • Attach to a pin or barrette
  • Use fabric glue to add to tote bags or clothing
