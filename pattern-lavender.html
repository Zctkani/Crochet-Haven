Lavender Crochet Pattern

Materials Needed

  • Light purple yarn (cotton or acrylic)
  • Green yarn for stem
  • Crochet hook (2.5mm – 3.5mm recommended)
  • Yarn needle, scissors

Instructions

Flower (make 4–5 strands):
R1: Chain 26
R2: In 2nd ch from hook, work *(sl st, ch 3, sl st)* in each chain across
Fasten off and leave a tail for attaching.

Stem:
With green yarn, ch 30 (or desired length)
Sl st in each ch back to beginning
Fasten off and weave in ends

Assembly:
Roll the purple flower strand into a spiral and sew the bottom using yarn needle
Attach the spiral flower to top of the stem with green yarn

Tips

  • You can make multiple stems for a bouquet effect.
  • Use floral wire inside the stem for stiffness.
  • Spray with lavender-scented oil for an aromatic touch!
