Crochet Cherry Keychain

  • Step 1: Create a magic ring, CH 1.
  • Step 2: Round 1: 6 SC into ring. (6 sts)
  • Step 3: Round 2: INC in each st around. (12 sts)
  • Step 4: Round 3: (SC in next st, INC) repeat around. (18 sts)
  • Step 5: Round 4: SC in each stitch. (18 sts)
  • Step 6: Round 5: (SC in next st, DEC) repeat around. (12 sts)
  • Step 7: Round 6: DEC around to close. Stuff before closing completely. (6 sts)
  • Step 8: Create 2 cherries, and stitch together.
  • Step 9: Crochet a small green stem: CH 10, SL ST back. Repeat for the second stem and attach both.
  • Step 10: Add keychain ring or clasp to top and secure.
