Granny Square Pattern

  • Round 1: Chain 4 and join with a slip stitch to form a ring. Chain 3 (counts as 1 DC), make 2 DC in the ring, *chain 2, 3 DC in ring* (repeat 3 times), chain 2 and slip stitch to the top of the first chain-3.
  • Round 2: Slip stitch to the first corner space. Chain 3, 2 DC, chain 2, 3 DC (first corner made). *In next corner space, 3 DC, chain 2, 3 DC.* Repeat for all corners. Join with a slip stitch.
  • Round 3: Continue working sets of 3 DC in each space and chain 2 in corners. Add rounds until desired size is reached.
  • Fasten off and weave in ends.
