3D Crochet Flower Coaster

  • Round 1: With center color (white), make a magic ring. Ch 2 (counts as 1 dc), work 11 dc in ring. Join. (12 dc)
  • Round 2: Ch 2, dc in same st, 2 dc in each st around. Join. (24 dc)
  • Round 3: Change to green. Ch 1, (sc, ch 3, skip 1) around. Join. (12 ch-3 spaces)
  • Round 4: In each ch-3 space: (sc, hdc, dc, hdc, sc) to form leafy petals.
  • Round 5: Attach petal color (e.g. red, yellow). From behind each leaf, ch 4, sl st behind next leaf. Repeat around. (12 ch-4 spaces)
  • Round 6: In each ch-4 space: (2 dc cluster, ch 1, 2 dc cluster), sl st to next space. This forms puffy petals. (12 3D petals)
  • Optional Round 7: Repeat previous step with another layer of chains behind current petals for a fuller flower.
  • Finish: Fasten off and weave in ends. Block if needed.
