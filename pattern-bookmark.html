Sakura Bookmark

  • Step 1: With pink yarn, make a magic ring and do 5 SC into the ring.
  • Step 2: In each stitch: (CH 3, DC, TR, DC, CH 3, SL ST) to form 5 petals.
  • Step 3: Fasten off and weave in the ends of the flower securely.
  • Step 4: Attach yarn to the back center of the flower and chain about 80–100 depending on desired length.
  • Step 5: At the end of the chain, create a small circle or mini bud with (magic ring, 6 SC), then join and fasten off.
  • Optional: Make a second flower or button on the opposite end for a wrap-around bookmark.
