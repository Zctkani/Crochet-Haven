Mini Crochet Bouquet

  • Step 1: Make 5–7 mini roses using magic ring:
    – MR, (ch 2, 2 DC, ch 2, sl st) × 5 around. Pull tight and sew closed.
  • Step 2: Create green leaves:
    – Ch 4, sl st in 2nd ch from hook, SC, HDC, fasten off.
  • Step 3: For the bouquet base:
    – MR, 6 SC in ring
    – 2 SC in each st (12)
    – SC around for 3-4 rounds
    – Dec every few sts to shape the cup.
  • Step 4: Make white frill top:
    – Join white yarn to top edge. (Ch 3, sl st in next st) around.
  • Step 5: Make the bouquet base or round back if desired (for flat bouquet).
  • Step 6: Arrange roses and leaves inside the cup or on the flat round. Sew securely.
  • Step 7: Attach a chain or keyring to turn it into a keychain.
