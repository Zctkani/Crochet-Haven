Mini 3D Crochet Heart

  • Round 1: Make 2 – Magic ring, 6 sc into ring (6)
  • Round 2: inc in each st around (12)
  • Round 3: (1 sc, inc) x 6 (18)
  • Round 4–5: sc in each st around (18)
  • Fasten off one piece. Do not cut the second one – we will join them now.
  • Round 6: sc around the first piece, then sc around the second piece (36)
  • Round 7: (4 sc, dec) x 6 (30)
  • Round 8: (3 sc, dec) x 6 (24)
  • Round 9: (2 sc, dec) x 6 (18)
  • Round 10: (1 sc, dec) x 6 (12)
  • Stuff the heart gently.
  • Round 11: dec x 6 (6)
  • Fasten off and sew the hole closed.
  • Shape it a little and you're done!
