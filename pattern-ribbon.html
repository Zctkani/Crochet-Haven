Crochet Ribbon Keychain

  • Step 1: Chain 25 (or desired length of ribbon tail).
  • Step 2: Turn, SC in second chain from hook, and across (24 SC).
  • Step 3: Continue SC rows (around 5–6 rows) to create a thick strip.
  • Step 4: Fold strip in half and sew the center together to form a bow shape.
  • Step 5: Wrap a short chain or band around the center to pinch it in place and fasten securely.
  • Step 6: You can add a contrasting band color for style (wrap with white or pastel yarn).
  • Step 7: Attach a small chain or keyring to the back or corner.
